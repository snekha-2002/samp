Beach or Mountain?

Do you get confused when you have to choose between beach and
mountain vacation? Well, we all have our preferences, but choosing
one out of these becomes a bit difficult. Well, you can visit places in
India that offer the best of both the world. If you are intrigued,here
are our favourite destinations that you can check out.

SWITZERLAND

Switzerland may be famous for many things-Enjoying wintertime adventures in the Swiss Alps is undoubtedly high on the bucket list of many an outdoor enthusiast,but there’s wonderful sightseeing, scenic hiking, and even taking to the skies to enjoy when the stunning Swiss landscape is without snow. Here we show you the kind of fun Switzerland has to offer in every season.

MALDIVES SPOTS

The Maldives is a dream holiday destination with its amazingly clear waters, gorgeous weather and over-water cabins on stilts. We’ve got 10 facts about the Maldives to share with you about this little slice of paradise!Scenery aside, the Maldives has a rich culture and fascinating history, along with unique geology with the whole country being made up of 26 coral atolls that formed after the eruption of ancient underwater volcanoes.

LONDON SPOTS

The largest city in both England and the United Kingdom is London.If you consider visiting this wonderful city, you must see these top London attractions. Here we have described some popular tourist places in London Some of the more popular and most visited places in London by tourists would have to be Regent's Park, Royal Opera House, and Houses of Parliament.
